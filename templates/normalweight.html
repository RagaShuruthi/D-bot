YOU ARE AT YOUR NORMAL WEIGHT

Maintain your healthy lifestyle with these tips!

Recommended Diet Plan

Breakfast

Option 1: 2 Idlies or 2 dosa and one egg with vegetables (as your wish) and a glass of milk.
Option 2: A plate of upma (150g) or kichadi (150g) and one egg and a glass of fruit juice.
Option 3: 2 Chappathies with paneer or chicken and a protein shake (dry fruits, banana, and milk).
Option 4: A ragi dosa or other millet dosa (2), a fruit, and sprouts.

Lunch

Option 1: A bowl of white rice with sambar or any curry with a cup of vegetables.
Option 2: A bowl of white rice with chicken and a piece of fish.
Option 3: Red rice with veggie curry and an egg.
Option 4: Wheat roti with chicken or veggies and your favorite fruit juice.

Dinner

Option 1: 2 Idlies or 2 dosa with one egg and a glass of milk.
Option 2: A plate of upma (150g) or kichadi (150g) with one egg and fruit juice.
Option 3: 2 Chappathies with paneer or chicken and a protein shake.
Option 4: A ragi dosa or other millet dosa (2), a fruit, and sprouts.

Exercise Recommendations

  • 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio (e.g., jogging or swimming).
  • Include yoga sessions twice a week for flexibility and mindfulness.
  • Strength training 2-3 times a week (bodyweight exercises or light weights).
  • Aim for at least 8,000-10,000 steps per day.
