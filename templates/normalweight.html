Recommended Diet Plan

Breakfast

  • Option 1: 2 Idlies or 2 dosa, one egg, vegetables, and a glass of milk.
  • Option 2: A plate of upma (150g) or kichadi (150g), one egg, and fruit juice.
  • Option 3: 2 Chappathies with paneer or chicken and a protein shake.
  • Option 4: A bowl of oats with nuts and a glass of buttermilk.

Lunch

  • Option 1: A bowl of white rice with sambar or curry with vegetables.
  • Option 2: A bowl of white rice with chicken and a piece of fish.
  • Option 3: Red rice with veggie curry and an egg.
  • Option 4: Millet rice with lentil soup and steamed veggies.

Dinner

  • Option 1: 2 Idlies or 2 dosa, one egg, and a glass of milk.
  • Option 2: A plate of upma (150g) or kichadi (150g) and fruit juice.
  • Option 3: 2 Chappathies with paneer or chicken and a protein shake.
  • Option 4: A bowl of vegetable soup with a multigrain bread slice.

Exercise Recommendations

  • 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio (e.g., jogging or swimming).
  • Include yoga sessions twice a week for flexibility and mindfulness.
  • Strength training 2-3 times a week (bodyweight exercises or light weights).
  • Aim for at least 8,000-10,000 steps per day.
