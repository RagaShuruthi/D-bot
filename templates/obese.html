Diet and Exercise Plan

Take steps towards a healthier lifestyle with these recommendations!

Recommended Diet Plan

  • Morning: Start with a glass of lukewarm lemon water and a bowl of oatmeal with fresh berries.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken or fish with steamed vegetables and a small portion of brown rice or quinoa.
  • Evening Snack: A handful of mixed nuts or a low-fat yogurt.
  • Dinner: A large bowl of vegetable soup or salad with lean protein (chicken breast or tofu).
  • Hydration: Drink at least 8-10 glasses of water throughout the day.

Exercise Recommendations

  • Start with 30 minutes of brisk walking or light jogging daily.
  • Include yoga or Pilates twice a week for flexibility and relaxation.
  • Strength training 2-3 times a week (focus on bodyweight exercises like squats and push-ups).
  • Incorporate low-impact cardio like cycling or swimming.
  • Try fun activities like dance or Zumba to stay active.
