Diet and Exercise Plan

Your guide to healthy weight gain.

Recommended Diet Plan

  • Morning: A glass of full-fat milk, nuts (almonds, cashews), and a banana.
  • Lunch: Whole grains (brown rice, quinoa), lean proteins (chicken, tofu), and vegetables.
  • Evening Snack: A peanut butter sandwich or a protein smoothie.
  • Dinner: Sweet potato, grilled fish, or paneer curry with whole-grain bread.
  • Before Bed: A glass of milk with honey or dry fruits.

Exercise Recommendations

  • Focus on strength training (e.g., weightlifting).
  • Bodyweight exercises like push-ups, squats, and planks.
  • Low-intensity cardio like brisk walking or cycling for 20-30 minutes.
  • Include yoga for flexibility and relaxation.
